Chicken and Broccoli Stir Fry

Homepage

Link to original recipe

Description

“

This chicken and broccoli recipe is easy to make by stir-frying chicken pieces with fresh broccoli in a mix of soy, ginger, garlic, and brown sugar. Serve with rice for an easy, pleasing dinner.

”

Ingredients

  • Oil: neutral oil like canola oil heated in a skillet
  • Chicken: a pound of boneliess chicken thighs cut into pieces
  • Cornstarch: for coating the chicken, esuring a crispy exterior
  • Onion: yellow onion is recommended; not too sweet
  • Ginger and garlic: to take the flavor up a notch
  • Broccoli: fresh is best but frozen works
  • Water: start the sauce with 3/4 cups of water
  • Sauces: soy sauce and/or oyster sauce are essential for this savory stir-fry
  • Brown sugar: sweeten things up with a tablespoon of light brown sugar
  • Seasonings: season the stir-fry sauce with crushed red pepper and black pepper

Directions

  1. Coat the chicken in cornstarch, then cook until crisp on all sides in hot oil
  2. Add the onions, ginger, and garlic and cook. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Cook the broccoli in the skillet
  4. Meanwhile, make the sauce
  5. Add the chicken mixture to the broccoli, then add the soy sauce mixture
  6. Cook until the chicken and broccoli are well-coated and the sauce is thickened
